Phase 2 : Technical Preparation

After preparing your company-specific resume and CV, gear up your technical skills for the technical round of the interview process.

Any company offering job placements will conduct 3 rounds of assessment , namely :

  1. Aptitude/MCQs round
  2. Technical round (coding questions)/group discussions
  3. Hiring interview (HR round)

MockIt

 Practice Common Interview Questions

MockIt offers a comprehensive suite of resources and mock questions to empower job seekers to confidently prepare for technical interviews. It provides detailed insights into each interview round, including:

  • Aptitude/MCQ Round :
    • Explanation of the round's purpose and question types.
    • Relevant subjects and topics covered.
    • Practice MCQs with detailed solutions.
  • Group Discussion (GD) Round :
    • Understanding the GD format and its significance.
    • Tips for effective participation in GDs.
    • Practice GD topics and scenarios.
  • Technical Round :
    • A curated list of essential technical topics.
    • In-depth learning resources and practice exercises for each topic.
    • Mock coding challenges to simulate real-world interview scenarios.
  • HR Round :
    • Common HR interview questions and effective response strategies.
    • Tips for making a strong impression during the HR round.

By leveraging MockIt's resources, job seekers can enhance their technical skills, refine their communication abilities, and increase their chances of success in technical interviews.
